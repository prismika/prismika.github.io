A good submission should be...

  • Updated every year around New Year's Day
  • Somewhat long-running
  • Not a person (such as "Teacher of the Year" or "TIME Person of the Year")
  • Definitive. There should not be multiple major versions of the same award. ("Song of the Year," "Word of the Year," etc.)
